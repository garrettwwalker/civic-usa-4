Form 4868 · 6-month extension

File a tax extension

Files Form 4868 with the IRS (and your state where required). You get six more months to file — but any tax owed is still due by April 15.

Extends your filing deadline to Oct 15, 2026. Does NOT extend the payment deadline — interest and penalties accrue on unpaid balances after April 15.

Confirmation by email within minutes.
